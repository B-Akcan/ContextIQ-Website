About ContextIQ

ContextIQ is a mobile and web application that acts as an intelligent assistant, helping non-technical team members get accurate, contextually relevant answers to their questions about technical documents without needing to interrupt technical staff.

By leveraging advanced AI and retrieval augmented generation (RAG) techniques, the application is able to understand user queries within the context of Mobiliz's products and services, providing immediate and accurate responses.

"Your Intelligent Document Companion"

Features

  • Real-Time, Accurate Responses

    Utilizes Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide up-to-date and precise answers by referencing the most relevant documents in real time.

  • Comprehensive Document Parsing

    Fully processes PDF documents, including text, tables, and lists, ensuring no detail is missed.

  • Powerful Document Management

    Upload documents through the admin page, enabling the LLM to learn from over 210 existing files and continuously expand its knowledge.

  • Context-Aware Conversations

    Remembers the context of previous questions, providing more relevant and coherent responses over time.

  • Focused Answers

    Select specific documents to guide the chatbot's responses, ensuring accurate and targeted answers.

  • Voice-Activated Interactions

    Ask your questions out loud with built-in speech recognition, making conversations faster and more natural.

  • Modern, Responsive Design

    Features a sleek, mobile-friendly interface for a smooth user experience on any device.
